About Us

Conveying Experience and Artistry at Tran Tattoo

Welcome to Tran Tattoo, where the art of tattooing blends seamlessly with your skin. Since our founding in 2013, we’ve built a team of talented and passionate artists who are dedicated to delivering unique and exceptional tattoo experiences. Our studio is a warm, welcoming space where you are free to express your personality through vivid tattoos—each one a true work of art.

At Tran Tattoo, we don’t just create tattoos—we tell stories. Each design is a part of you, carrying memories and personal meanings. Our artists specialize in everything from timeless classics to modern styles, helping you choose or co-create a fully personalized design that reflects your unique style.

Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. We use only premium-quality inks and materials, and strictly follow hygiene standards to ensure a clean and safe tattooing process. Our studio is equipped with modern tools and offers a relaxing environment for you to fully enjoy your tattoo experience.

Why choose Tran Tattoo?
At Tran Tattoo, we stand out with our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our experienced artists don’t just create beautiful tattoos—they craft meaningful pieces that truly reflect your personality and style. With top-tier equipment and a strict hygiene protocol, you can trust us every step of the way.

We offer a friendly, open space where you’re encouraged to share your ideas and inspirations. With passion and dedication, we’ll help bring your vision to life through personalized artwork—turning each tattoo into a memorable milestone in your life’s journey.

Personalized Tattoo Experience at Tran Tattoo
Whether it’s your first tattoo or a new piece in your collection, Tran Tattoo is here to walk with you. We’re more than just a tattoo studio—we’re a place where your ideas become living art. Our creative and dedicated team is committed to delivering visually stunning and meaningful tattoos.

At Tran Tattoo, every tattoo is a journey, a story told through ink and needle. We focus on every detail, creating unique and expressive pieces that allow you to carry a piece of art and culture with you—proudly and meaningfully.
