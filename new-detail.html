Deauville Tattoo Festival

Our tattoo studio is proud to have received numerous prestigious awards in the industry, recognizing our commitment to artistry and customer satisfaction. We were honored to receive the "Best Tattoo Studio" award at the National Tattoo Convention, celebrating the exceptional skill and creativity of our talented artists. In addition, our innovative designs earned the "People's Choice Award" in 2024, reflecting the support and appreciation of our loyal clients. These achievements inspire us to continue pushing the boundaries of tattoo artistry while maintaining a welcoming and professional space for every customer who visits us.

The Journey to Recognition

This award is the result of a passionate journey filled with creativity and relentless effort to perfect the art of tattooing. At Tattoo Trần, every tattoo is not just artwork on skin—it is a personal story told through ink and emotion. We pour our hearts and dedication into each stroke, blending intricate details with bold colors to create truly unique impressions. From the first sketch to the final needle stroke, our goal has always been to deliver not just a tattoo—but a vivid, meaningful memory that accompanies you throughout your life journey.

The Significance of This Award to Us

We are incredibly proud to share that Tattoo Trần has won Second Prize in the Traditional category at the Deauville Tattoo Festival 2024 —one of Europe’s most prestigious tattoo art events. This award is not just a trophy; it’s a milestone that affirms the passionate and persistent journey we’ve pursued for many years.

This achievement reflects the trust of our clients, the recognition from industry professionals, and above all, our deep love for traditional Vietnamese tattoo art. We extend our heartfelt thanks to the judges, international peers, and especially to those who have continuously supported and believed in Tattoo Trần. We will keep pushing creative boundaries and learning, striving to elevate Vietnamese tattoo artistry on the global stage.

Celebrating Art – Thank You for Being a Part of Our Journey

To mark this proud milestone, Tattoo Trần will be hosting a special event right at our studio – a vibrant gathering where art and community come together. We warmly invite all clients, friends, and tattoo enthusiasts to join us for a day filled with emotion, creativity, and meaningful connection.

The celebration will feature live tattoo sessions, thoughtful giveaways, and the rare opportunity to interact with the passionate artists behind each masterpiece. This is not merely a party – it is our heartfelt way of expressing gratitude to those who have inspired and empowered us throughout Tattoo Trần’s artistic journey.

The Road Ahead – Relentless in Creativity

Looking ahead, we carry the same burning passion in our hearts and an unwavering commitment to innovation. At Tattoo Trần, we pledge to deliver increasingly personalized, visually striking, and meaningful tattoo experiences—crafted with precision, intention, and bold expression.

We are excited to explore new techniques and expand the language of our artistry, ensuring that every tattoo you wear is not just ink on skin, but a deeply personal and distinctive work of art. Thank you for continuing this journey with us—let’s write the next inspiring chapter of this artistic adventure together.
